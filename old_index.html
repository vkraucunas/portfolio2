About Valerie

After reaching my limit of answering phones and unjamming copy machines as an administrative assistant, I enrolled in the Galvanize Full Stack Immersive program. While in the program, I built my confidence in solving problems with code, worked with groups, and done one of my favorite things: CREATE!

As a developer, I have front-end leanings, but value the process of building a whole application from drawing an entity relationship diagram for the database to finalizing design. I'm highly organized; so is my code and my approach to coding.

Have doubts about how much someone can learn in 6 months? Here is the portfolio site I put together as pre-work for Galvanize.

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my dogs and cat, playing video games, pinning, and cooking.
