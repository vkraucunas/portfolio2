About

My Career Transition

After reaching my limit of answering phones and unjamming copy machines as an administrative assistant, I enrolled in the Galvanize Full Stack Immersive program. While in the program, I built my confidence in solving problems with code, worked with groups, and done one of my favorite things: CREATE!

What I Add to a Team

As a developer, I have front-end leanings, but value the full stack. My experience to this point has been focused on web development, and most of my professional work has been done in React.js.

  • Strong communication skills and positive attitude make me fun to know and work with. I strive to be always adding value, even if it is as simple as adding more emojis to the company Slack team.
  • Dedication to documentation and sharing knowledge across teams.
  • Familiarity with best practices of how to bring junior talent into a development team.
  • Excitement to accept challenges and progressively solve more difficult problems over time.
  • User-focused development; the experience of the user drives my work. I will speak up especially about UI accessibility.
  • Always looking for ways to improve my code contributions.
