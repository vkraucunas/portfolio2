About Valerie

After reaching my limit of answering phones and unjamming copy machines as an administrative assistant, I enrolled in the Galvanize Full Stack Immersive program. While in the program, I built my confidence in solving problems with code, worked with groups, and done one of my favorite things: CREATE!

As a developer, I have front-end leanings, but value the process of building a whole application from drawing an entity relationship diagram for the database to finalizing design. I'm highly organized; so is my code and my approach to coding.

Have doubts about how much someone can learn in 6 months? Here is the portfolio site I put together as pre-work for Galvanize.

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my dogs and cat, playing video games, pinning, and cooking.


Development Projects

Alligator Jobs

App designed with a team to help refugees find work in America and erase the divide between different working cultures. Our application creates an easy to use platform for job seekers, their caseworkers and potential employers to connect on skills job seekers have instead of a job history they may not.

Build

HTML5, CSS3, Swig, Bootstrap, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, Knex.js, Google translate, Google authorization, Local authorization, Cookies, Encryption

Impressions

Yelp-like application, specifically for fictional restaurants. Impressions allows visitors to view restaurants' information and user reviews. Users can leave reviews for the restaurants and edit old reviews.

Build

HTML5, CSS3, Swig, Bootstrap, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, Knex.js, Facebook Authorization

DINK Kitchen

"DINK Kitchen", as in "Dual Income No Kids", is a dinner selector for people who like to cook. It is front-end only, using a Trello board as a data source. It also calls to OpenWeatherMap to check for local weather and suggest weather-suitable recipes.

Build

HTML5, CSS3, Swig, Bootstrap, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, Knex.js, Facebook Authorization
