About

After reaching my limit of answering phones and unjamming copy machines as an administrative assistant, I enrolled in the Galvanize Full Stack Immersive program. While in the program, I have been able to build my confidence in solving problems with code, work with groups, and do one of my favorite things: CREATE!

As a developer, I definitely have some front-end leanings, but value the process of building a whole application from drawing an entity relationship diagram for the database to finalizing design. I'm highly organized and so is my code and my approach to coding.

Have doubts about how much someone can learn in 6 months? Here is the portfolio site I put together as pre-work for Galvanize.

In my free time, I enjoy spending time with my dogs and cat, playing video games, pinning, and cooking.



Development Projects

Alligator Jobs

App designed with a team to help refugees find work in America and erase the divide between different working cultures. Our application creates an easy to use platform for job seekers, their caseworkers and potential employers to connect on skills job seekers have instead of a job history they may not.

Build

HTML5, CSS3, Swig, Bootstrap, Node.js, Express, PSQL, Knex.js, Google translate, Google authorization, Local authorization, Cookies, Encryption
